Printed tables from Weatherrescue Ben Nevis project¶

Ben Nevis project sample table - half a page.

The ‘Meteorology of Ben Nevis’ project published 1.6 million weather observations in the Transactions of the Royal Society of Edinburgh - around 2000 pages of printed tables.

These observations have already been transcribed, by the Weather rescue Ben Nevis project, and this makes them a great choice for testing new transcription methods. We have combined the published images and transcribed data to make a validation dataset.

The UK Daily Weather Reports (1862)
===================================

.. figure:: ../../../samples/DWR_1862_03.jpg
   :width: 75%
   :align: center
   :figwidth: 95%

   UK Daily Weather report from 1862 sample image

The `Daily Weather Reports <http://brohan.org/UK-digitisation/sources/DWR.html>`_ are collections of telegraph observations used to make daily synoptic weathermaps. The UK started producing them in 1860, and they started out as a simple printed table with handwritten entries.
